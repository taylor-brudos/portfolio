Why I Code

Programming software is akin to spelunking (pictured left: that's me at Waitomo Caves in New Zealand). There's the thrill of descending deep into uncharted territories as you begin your expedition, you may trip or lose your way in the dark, and so long as you keep pushing forward you will make it to the exhilarating light at the end of your journey.

What I Code

My first experience with "coding" was creating complex Excel formulas at my job as an accountant. After working in finance for a while, I came to realize that there is a huge opportunity to incorporate coding with the finance world in order to automate mundane and manual processes. That realization catipulted me into a coding bootcamp where I became a Full-Stack Developer.

How I Code

At the core, I am a Full-Stack Developer. I build my apps using Python, JavaScript, and Java and incorporate libraries including openpyxl, python-docx, Ajax, jQuery, D3.js, and CanvasJS. I am always looking for new, innovative tools to incorporate into my projects.
